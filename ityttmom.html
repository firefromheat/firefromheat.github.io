i think you think too much of me - eden (2016)


8/10

Alternative Rock / Indie / Electronic

Favourite Track - fumes

Least Favourite Track - and

"i think you think too much of me" is Irish artist eden's second solo studio album, and has a vibe/feeling that I can only really describe as somber but hopeful.

"fumes" and "xo" are both seemingly sad/somber songs but also have a feeling of happiness hiding in the background.

My only issue is “and,” as it just falls flat comparatively to the other tracks. It’s not a bad song by any means, just nowhere close to the others on here.


Great album overall. eden has successfully created a sound of his own that nobody else has, and it seem's like he's mastered it in this release.
