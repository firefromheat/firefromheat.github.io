From Me To You - Quadeca (2021)


9/10

Alternative Rap

"Favourite Track - Sisyphus"

"Least Favourite Track - WHERE'D YOU GO"

Beautiful album. It revolves around a theme of climbing a mountain, whether that be a metaphorical one or a real one.

Songs like “Smiling At The Ground” and “Sisyphus” are beautifully crafted pieces of art that I have both cried to, while songs like “Work!” and “Burnin Bridges/Long Day” are bangers that you can play at practically any time.

The worst track on this album, "WHERE'D YOU GO" isn't even a bad song overall, it just doesn't fit with the album's themes.

Overall, I really enjoyed this album and I recommend that you give it a try!
